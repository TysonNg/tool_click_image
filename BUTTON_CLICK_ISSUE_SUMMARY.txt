IF ALL BLUE → FIX CLICK POINT (3 phút)
================================================================================

1. Open AutoClick
2. Load Arena scenario
3. Right-click first image → "Edit"
4. Click "Pick Click Point"
5. Click CENTER (giữa) của blue button
6. Save
7. Repeat for all images
8. Close and open AutoClick
9. Test

================================================================================
IF ANY YELLOW → RECAPTURE (5-10 phút)
================================================================================

1. Delete yellow images (Edit → Delete)
2. Go to Dragon City
3. Get to Arena screen, before clicking "Go to Menu"
4. Open AutoClick
5. Click "Add Image"
6. Drag to crop ONLY the blue button
   - Don't include yellow button nearby
   - Include whole blue button
   - Don't leave out any part
7. Release
8. Click "Pick Click Point"
9. Click CENTER of button
10. Save
11. Close and open AutoClick
12. Test
